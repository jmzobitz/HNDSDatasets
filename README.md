# Sources of community datasets for DST 490

- [USDA county level datasets](https://ers.usda.gov/data-products/county-level-data-sets/county-level-data-sets-download-data)
  - Downloadable in Excel
  - **Latest data release:** 2023
  - **Last checked**: 13 Jan 2026
  - Datasets located in `data-raw`
  
- [Minnesota Student Survey](https://education.mn.gov/mde/dse/health/mss/)
  - Need to submit a data request.
  
- [Open Data Minneapolis](https://opendata.minneapolismn.gov/)

- [County Health Tables](https://www.health.mn.gov/data/index.html)

- [Minnesota Geospatial Commons](https://gisdata.mn.gov/)

- [Minneapolis Fed Research](https://www.minneapolisfed.org/research)

- [American Community Survey](https://www.census.gov/programs-surveys/acs/data.html)

- [MN Dept of Education](https://pub.education.mn.gov/MDEAnalytics/Data.jsp)

- [HRSA Data](https://data.hrsa.gov/topics/health-centers/uds#Downloads)

- [Bureau of Labor Statistics](https://www.bls.gov/oes/tables.htm)

- [Food Group / Hunger & Food Security Data](https://www.thefoodgroupmn.org/news/hunger-data/)

- [Global Health Observatory (WHO)](https://www.who.int/data/gho)
  International health indicators, mortality trends, and disease statistics.

- [HealthData.gov](https://healthdata.gov)
  Open datasets covering public health, hospitals, diseases, and healthcare operations.

- [CMS Data](https://data.cms.gov)
  Massive US healthcare datasets - costs, providers, outcomes, claims.

- [re3data](https://www.re3data.org)
  A global registry of research data repositories. Search thousands of health datasets.
